Invoice discounting provides short-duration debt returns that are typically uncorrelated with equity and traditional fixed income markets. The category offers attractive yields, but platform quality varies significantly.

We identify high-quality originator platforms, assess underlying debtor concentration, and help allocate a considered portion of the fixed income sleeve toward this yield-enhancing category, sized appropriately to overall risk.
