## Long-term orientation

We measure success in decades, not quarters. Markets reward patience; our role is to help you stay invested through the discomfort that makes returns possible. Short-term noise is expected. We help you treat it as such.

## Unbiased advice

We earn no commissions on product sales. Our business model is built on advice, not distribution. This removes the single largest source of conflict in financial services, and it shapes every conversation we have with you.

## Simplicity over complexity

Complexity is often sold as sophistication. In our experience, simple, well-understood portfolios consistently outperform elaborate structures that obscure true risk. We explain everything in plain language, and we never recommend what we cannot explain clearly.

## Risk before return

Every conversation begins with loss scenarios, not return projections. A portfolio you can remain committed to during a drawdown is worth more than an optimal portfolio you abandon at the worst possible moment.

## Education as an ongoing practice

An informed investor makes better decisions. We invest time in ensuring you understand not just what we recommend, but why. Our goal is to make you less dependent on us over time, not more.
